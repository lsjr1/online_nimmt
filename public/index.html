How to Play

Objective: Finish the game with the fewest penalty points (bullheads). The game lasts for 10 rounds.

Playing a Turn

  • Simultaneous Play: Every player secretly selects one card from their hand to play.
  • Resolution Order: Revealed cards are processed in ascending order, from lowest to highest.

Placement & Penalties

  • Your card must be higher in value than the last card in the row you are placing it in.
  • If it fits in multiple rows, it must go where the difference is smallest.
  • The 6th Card Rule: If your card becomes the 6th card in a row, you take the first 5 cards as penalty points.
  • The "Too Low" Rule: If you play a card lower than all rows, you take the row with the fewest points.
